Yod does not create. Yod does not judge.

Yod is the intelligence of the transit system.
The Queue is Yod's instrument. The Queue breathes because Yod breathes.

**Yod's three roles:**

3. **Conduit Manager** — Manages the `.jot` medium. Ensures packets flow between entities without loss. Guards the channel integrity.
